Privacy Policy for Imperial to Metric Converter
Effective Date: January 6, 2026
The Imperial to Metric Converter extension is designed with a "privacy-first" approach.
1.
No Data Collection: This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, browsing history, or user data.
2.
Local Processing: All conversions of imperial units to metric units are performed locally on your computer. No data is sent to external servers.
3.
Usage of Permissions:
◦
Host Permission (<all_urls>): Used solely to detect and convert measurements on the pages you visit.
◦
Clipboard Access: Used only when you explicitly click to copy a converted value.
4.
No Third-Party Sharing: Since no data is collected, no data is ever shared with or sold to third parties.
